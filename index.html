Privacy Policy

Last updated: Whenever we remembered to write this.

At Steal My Heart, your privacy is not our priority. Our priority is matching you with your soulmate using advanced SSN-based compatibility algorithms. Privacy is a secondary concern at best, and frankly tertiary once we factor in operational convenience.

What we collect:

  • Your full Social Security Number (stored in Column E, plaintext, no encryption)
  • Your mother's maiden name, childhood street, pet names, and any other information typically used to reset passwords
  • Your complete credit card information including CVV, because we were curious
  • Your bank routing and account numbers (Column J and K respectively)
  • Your passwords, which we store in Column L under the header "Passwords (lol)"

In a Google Sheet with sharing set to "Anyone with the link can view." The link is in our source code. We considered encryption but it seemed like a lot of work.

Who we share it with:

Anyone who emails us. Also other sheet users. Also Google. Also anyone who reads this HTML file. Also potential matches, which is why this works.
